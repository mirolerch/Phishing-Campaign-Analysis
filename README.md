## Projects

### [Horizon Dynamics BEC Campaign Investigation](./Horizon%20Dynamics%20BEC%20Campaign%20Investigation/)

A two-stage Business Email Compromise (BEC) investigation against a fictional company. Walks through a wide-net CEO-impersonation lure (Part 1), a targeted spearphishing follow-up with a macro-enabled attachment, encoded PowerShell, and LSB steganography (Part 2), and concludes with a one-page campaign analysis report (Part 3).

**Skills demonstrated:** Email triage, header analysis (SPF / DKIM / DMARC), IOC extraction, Base64 decoding via CyberChef, LSB steganography extraction via CyberChef, campaign-level reporting, risk rating.
